The Process

How It Works

From your first conversation to a fully implemented plan — here's exactly what to expect.

01

Choose Your Service or Book a Consultation

Clients either book a consultation or select the service that fits their current needs. If you're not sure where to start, a consultation is always the right first move. We'll walk through your situation together and identify the best path forward.

Book Now
02

Receive Onboarding

After purchase, you receive onboarding details, next steps, and any required instructions — delivered digitally the same day. For purchases made after 5 PM EST, onboarding begins the next business day. Everything you need to get started comes directly to you.

03

Schedule or Access the Service

You receive access to your consulting session, program materials, resources, or support process. Depending on the service selected, this may include a scheduled call, digital materials, or access to your full consulting program.

04

Build the Game Plan

We review your business, goals, challenges, and current systems to create a clear plan of action. You walk away with a structured direction — not vague advice — but specific next steps and priorities mapped out for your business.

05

Implementation Support

Depending on the service, we provide guidance, resources, and support to help you execute the plan. For clients in ongoing advisory programs, this includes continued access to consulting support as you build, adjust, and grow over time.
